# How‑to: Auditing SOPs

Auditing SOPs ensures compliance with governance policies and external stakeholder requirements.  
It strengthens accountability, transparency, and donor confidence.

## Verify Compliance
Check adherence to internal governance rules and external standards.

## Align Donor Reporting
Establish audit cycles (quarterly, annual) aligned with ISO and donor reporting timelines.

## Diagnostics
Use diagnostic checklists to assess reproducibility, clarity, and operational consistency.

## Identify & Recommend
Highlight gaps, risks, or deviations and recommend corrective actions.

## Transparency
Document audit findings with version control for transparency and sponsor optics.

## Audit Results
Share results with governance committees, donors, and operations teams to reinforce accountability.

## Why This Matters

- **For Nonprofits**: SOPs safeguard transparency, reproducibility, and donor confidence.  
- **For ITIL Practitioners**: SOPs align with service management processes, ensuring consistency and audit readiness.  
- **For Sponsors**: SOPs demonstrate accountability and sustainable programme delivery.  
- **For Everyone**: SOPs make services clearer, safer, and easier to use.
